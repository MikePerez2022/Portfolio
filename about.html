About Me

I’m a developer passionate about both software and game development, with a strong interest in dynamic systems, AI, and emerging technologies.

My Capstone project, Boundless, focused on creating advanced parkour mechanics in Unreal Engine using both C++ and Blueprints. I love designing movement systems and refining gameplay feel through iteration.

At iAMAi, I contributed to an open-source AI platform by building a clean and responsive frontend in React.js, and helped develop a Unity plugin for integrating local AI brain logic using DLLs and backend research.

I'm always eager to collaborate on projects that are meaningful, challenging, and innovative—whether in the world of interactive games or impactful software solutions.

Skills: Unreal Engine, Unity, C++, C#, JavaScript, React.js, Git, Blueprints, AI Systems
